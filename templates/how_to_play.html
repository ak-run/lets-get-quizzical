How to Play

  1. First enter your nickname on the home page and pick your Avatar
  2. When you are ready click Start Quiz
  3. Next select a category from the list, Once you have done this you will be taken straight to the game so be ready!
  4. Answer each multiple choice question before the timer runs out, If it does you will not get a point
  5. Click the next button on the timer to go to the next question
  6. Go through all 10 questions and see your score, your answers and the correct answers at the end
  7. Next submit your score to the leaderboard. The top 10 scores will be displayed
